# Design System: Consultoria Construção Financiada
**Project ID:** 11167260276246442545

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The core aesthetic is **"The Architectural Monolith."** It treats the digital interface as an engineered landscape, conveying high authority through expansive negative space, oversized technical typography, and a depth-first hierarchy. It utilizes intentional asymmetry and tonal transitions rather than traditional borders to define structure. The vibe is sophisticated, technical, and premium.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
The palette is rooted in a "void-black" foundation with high-contrast accent colors.

*   **Void Black (#131313):** The base canvas, providing a deep, authoritative background.
*   **Engineering Orange (#FF6B00):** The primary brand engine, used for critical actions and highlight accents.
*   **Technical Peach (#FFB693):** A secondary textural accent used for labels and subtle iconography.
*   **Blueprint Blue (#026BE0):** Functional accent color representing technical precision and blue-sky thinking.
*   **Skyline Blue (#ADC7FF):** Used for interactive feedback and secondary informational elements.
*   **Recess Dark (#0E0E0E):** The deepest tonal layer, used for footers or recessed areas.
*   **Ghost Grey (#5A4136):** Used for blueprint grids and subtle structural hints.

## 3. Typography Rules
Uses a high-contrast technical pairing:
*   **Space Grotesk:** Primary font for headlines and technical technical metadata. Tracked tightly (-2%) for an authoritative look.
*   **Inter:** Primary font for functional body copy and labels. Prioritizes readability and technical clarity.

## 4. Component Stylings
*   **Buttons:** Sharp corners (`rounded-sm`: 0.125rem) to reflect structural rigidity. Primary buttons use the Engineering Orange background with all-caps Inter typography.
*   **Cards & Containers:** Defined through tonal shifts (`surface` to `surface_container_low`) rather than borders. High-end editorial feel with intentional vertical whitespace.
*   **The "No-Line" Rule:** 1px solid borders are strictly prohibited for sectioning. Boundaries must be defined through tonal layering.

## 5. Layout Principles
*   **Intentional Asymmetry:** Content is often weighted to one side to create a sense of momentum (e.g., left-aligned headlines with right-aligned CTAs).
*   **Tonal Layering:** Depth is achieved by stacking different shades of dark surfaces.
*   **Blueprint Grids:** Subtle SVG patterns of dots or thin lines are used in hero sections to reinforce the civil engineering theme.
*   **Massive Whitespace:** Sections are separated by large gaps (160px+) to allow the editorial content to breathe.
